Recipe For Pizza:

Hi and Welcome!

This is where you will learn how to make pizza whenever you want

with whatever you want.This is generically speaking a guide in which

it explains step by step and detail by detail how to make fresh pizza!

List Of All Necessary Ingredients:

  • 1+1/2 Cups of Warm Water(11 Degrees F/45 Degrees C)
  • 1 Teaspoon of White Sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon of Active Dry Yeast
  • 1 Tablespoon of Olive Oil
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 2 Cups of Whole Wheat Flour
  • 1+1/2 Cups of All-Purpose Flour

List Of All Complete Details:

  • Preparation Time: 25 Minutes
  • Additional Time: 1 Hour 55 Minutes
  • Total Time: 2 Hour 20 Minutes
  • Servings: 10
  • Yield: 1 Large Pizza Crust
  • Calories: 167
  • Fat: 2g
  • Carbs: 33g
  • Protein: 6g

List Of All Necessary Steps:

  1. -Pour Warm Water into a Large Bowl; Stir It in Sugar to Dissolve.
    2. -Sprinkle Yeast on Top and Let It Stand until It's Foamy, about 10 Minutes.
  2. -Stir Olive Oil and Salt into Yeast Mixture; Mix in Whole Wheat Flour and
    3. -1 Cup of All-Purpose Flour until the Dough Starts to Come Together.
  3. -Turn Dough out onto a Surface Floured with Remaining All-Purpose Flour;
    4. -Knead until All of the Flour Has Been Absorbed and Dough Becomes Smooth, About 10 Minutes.
  4. -Place Dough in an Oiled Bowl and Turn to Coat the Surface.Cover Loosely with a Towel;
    5. -Let Stand in a Warm Place until Doubled in Size, About 1 Hour.
  5. -Turn Dough out onto a Lightly Floured Surface;
    6. -Divide into 2 Pieces for 2 Thin Crusts, or Leave Whole to Make One Thick Crust.
  6. -Form Dough into a Tight Ball; Place Dough Back into an Oiled Bowl and Turn to Coat the Surface.
    7. -Cover Loosely with a Towel; Let Stand in a Warm Place until Doubled in Size, about 45 Minutes.
  7. -Grease a Large Pizza Pan.Roll a Ball of Dough with a Rolling Pin until It Will Not Stretch Any Further.
    8. -Then, Drape It over Both of Your Fists, and Gently Pull the Edges Outward, While Rotating Crust.
  8. -When the Circle Has Reached the Desired Size, Place on the Prepared Pizza Pan.
    9. -Then Bake and Add Any Desired Sauce or Topping That You Want.
