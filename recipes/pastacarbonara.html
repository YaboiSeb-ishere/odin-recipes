Recipe For Carbonara Pasta:

Hi and Welcome!

This is where you will learn how to make carbonara pasta whenever you want

with whatever you want.This is generically speaking a guide in which

it explains step by step and detail by detail how to make fresh carbonara pasta!

List Of All Necessary Ingredients:

  • Desired Amount of Olive Oil
  • Desired Amount of Cured Pork Cheek also Known as Guanciale
  • Desired Amount of Spaghetti
  • 3 Eggs
  • Desired Amount of Cheese
  • Desired Amount of Seasonings

List Of All Complete Details:

  • Preparation Time: 5 Minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 Minutes
  • Total Time: 20 Minutes
  • Servings: 4
  • Yield: Desired Portion Of Guanciale
  • Calories: 764
  • Fat: 28g
  • Carbs: 85g
  • Protein: 39g

List Of All Necessary Steps:

  1. -Cook the Pork in Olive Oil until Browned and Crispy, then Drain It on Paper Towls.
  2. -Boil the Spaghetti in Salted Water. Drain It and Return to the Pot. Let it cool.
  3. -Whisk the Eggs, 1/2 of the Cheese and Desired Pepper in a Bowl until Smooth.
  4. -Pour the Egg Mixture over The Pasta, Stirring Quickly, until Creamy.
  5. -Stir It the Pasta in the Pork, then Add the Remaining Cheese and Necessary Black Pepper.
  6. -Serve Immediately After Being Cooked.
